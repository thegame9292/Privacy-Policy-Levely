Privacy Policy - Levely

Effective date: January 21, 2026

Last updated: January 21, 2026

In short: Levely does NOT collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is completely protected.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Levely, the professional digital level app for Android. This Privacy Policy clearly and transparently explains how we process (or rather, how we DON'T process) your personal data.

Levely is developed with one fundamental principle: your privacy is sacred. We do not collect personal data, do not track your activities, and do not share any information with third parties.

2. Data We DO NOT Collect

Levely does NOT collect:

  • Name, surname, or other personal information
  • Email address or phone number
  • Geographic location or GPS data
  • IP address
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID, etc.)
  • Browsing history
  • Usage data or statistics
  • Photos or images from gallery
  • Contacts or address book
  • Payment details

3. Locally Stored Data

Levely stores some data exclusively on your device to allow the app to function correctly. This data is NEVER sent to external servers:

  • Measurement history: The last 20 measurements saved when you lock a reading
  • Calibration settings: Your device's calibration values
  • User preferences: Settings related to sounds and vibrations

This data remains on your device and is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app.

4. Permissions Required by the App

Levely requires the following Android permissions to function properly:

4.1 Motion Sensors (Accelerometer and Gyroscope)

  • Why: To detect the tilt of the device and display the measurements
  • When: Always, when the app is in use
  • Data collected: NONE - sensor data is processed locally and never stored or sent

4.2 Vibration

  • Why: To provide tactile feedback when the device is perfectly level
  • When: Only when activated in settings and the device is level
  • Data collected: NONE

4.3 Camera (for LED flashlight only - optional)

  • Why: To activate the LED flashlight in dark environments
  • When: Only when you manually press the flashlight button
  • Data collected: NONE - we do NOT take photos, do NOT record videos, do NOT access the gallery
  • Note: This permission is completely optional. If you don't grant this permission, the app works normally but without the flashlight feature

5. Internet Connection

Levely does NOT require any internet connection to function. The app works completely offline. We do not send or receive data from external servers.

6. Advertising and Analytics

Levely does NOT include:

  • Third-party advertising
  • Trackers or analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • Third-party SDKs for data collection
  • Cookies or similar technologies

7. Data Sharing with Third Parties

We do NOT share any data with third parties because we simply don't collect data to share.

8. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and is not transmitted, security depends on your Android device's security settings (screen lock, encryption, etc.).

We recommend that you:

  • Keep your device updated to the latest version of Android
  • Use a PIN, pattern, or fingerprint to protect the device
  • Download apps only from official sources like Google Play Store

9. Children's Privacy

Levely can be used by users of all ages. We do not intentionally collect personal data from minors under 13, nor from users of any age.

10. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no data to:

  • Access
  • Rectify
  • Delete
  • Export
  • Restrict

All locally stored data can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the app data from Android settings.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. In case of substantial changes, we will inform you through:

  • In-app notification
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this document

We recommend that you periodically check this page for any updates.

12. GDPR Compliance (European Privacy Regulation)

Levely is fully compliant with the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) of the European Union because:

  • It does not process users' personal data
  • It does not transfer data outside the EU
  • It does not require consent for personal data processing (because we don't collect it)
  • It does not need to appoint a Data Protection Officer (DPO)

We are committed to maximum transparency. If you have doubts about how the app works or how data is managed, please don't hesitate to contact us.
